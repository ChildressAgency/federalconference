Company CultureHappy employees create happy customers and we have the track record to prove it!

Federal Conference has been continuously growing since 2006 and has no plans to stop. We provide a fast-paced work environment accompanied by a great work/life balance. With engaged employees and opportunities to advance, Federal Conference is a great place to begin or continue your career.

As a Federal Conference employee you will become part of our family. It may sound like a cliche but as a growing small business, we mean it! We work hard together and play hard together. Our social committee is dedicated to planning lunches, group events, after work get-togethers and our famous Holiday Party. New committee members are always welcome to help create new and exciting events.

Still skeptical? Reach our to HR@Rederalconference.com and we will tell you all about it!

Employee Benefits

The perks of being a Federal Conference employee don't stop at medical coverage. We offer a full range of benefits to support you and eligible family members' financial, medical and overall well-being by providing the following benefit plans.

  • HMO and PPO Medical Plans
  • Dental
  • Vision
  • FSA and HRA
  • 401k with Safe Harbor Plan
  • Group Life Insurance
  • Voluntary Life Insurance
  • Supplemental Healthcare Benefits

Our "employee-first" focus has driven us to provide these stand-out benefits, most of which are unheard of in our industry!

  • Casual attire
  • 08:30 - 17:00 Monday through Friday work days
  • Employee Assistance and Travel Programs
  • Professional Development Assistance
  • 10 PTO days and 3 Sick days earned within your first year of employment
  • Begin earning PRO and Sick leave on your first day
  • 11 paid Holidays per year plus 1 Floating Holiday (of your choice) per calendar year
