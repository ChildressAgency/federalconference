What Makes Us DifferentWe're proud to do things others don't

Federal Conference's goal, as a forward-thinking full-service event planning and management company, is to relieve our clients of the logistical burden of events and allow them to focus on the content. We view ourselves as partners with our customers, our employees, our community and our environments. We aim to become a nationally recognized brand name, capitalizing on our in-depth industry experience and leveraging our industry relationships.

Our History and AwardsWe're proud to do things others don't

Federal Conference was created in 2006 to provide cost effective event planning and management options for government, corporate and association entities. Federal Conference's objective is to apply meticulous precision and skilled talent to event planning/management services for its clients. Federal Conference is located thirty miles south of Washington, DC with a presence in twenty-five Key Destination Cities across the united States. Also, our team of certified and trained event planning professional average tenure is over 7 years. We've received recognition as Top 5 Best Places to Work - Meetings and Conventions Magazine and raked #2 by Inc 500. Meaning you can count on us to be consistent and stable now and in the future.
